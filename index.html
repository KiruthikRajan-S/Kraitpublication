Welcome To KRAIT Book Publishing Company

Krait Book Publishing is a dynamic and inclusive publishing company dedicated to bringing stories and knowledge to life in every form. We offer a wide range of books including storybooks, history books, comics, coffee table books, recipe books, and regional language literature — available in print, audio, animated, and digital formats.

Whether you're looking to read, listen, or experience a book in a more immersive way, Krait ensures quality, creativity, and accessibility for readers of all ages. From traditional publishing to modern innovations, we make book discovery exciting and meaningful for everyone.
